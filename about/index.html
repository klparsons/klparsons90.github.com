Keith Parsons is a picture editor at the Telegraph, based in London. Before joining the Telegraph Keith worked as a freelance photographer, pursuing projects in Australia and Sub-Saharan Africa. Born in 1990, and originally from Australia, Keith has lived abroad for the past 5 years.
